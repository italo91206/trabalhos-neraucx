PRIVACY POLICY

INFORMATION

95TrafficSafety.com gathers two types of information about customers:

1. Data that customers provide through optional, voluntary registrations on our website and/or landing pages and in certain contests and sweepstakes;

2. Data that 95TrafficSafety.com gathers through aggregating tracking information derived mainly by tallying page views throughout our sites and/or pages and click through on our advertising.

The data we gather includes email correspondence, customer names, billing information, and contact information.

Email correspondence consists of email messages sent to any 95TrafficSafety.com email address. Any email received is retained and the email address is stored for use by 95 Traffic Safety Supply. Your email address is not released to any outside party unless specifically requested by you

Customer names consist of the first and last name of customers who inquire or place an order for a product. This information is retained for use by 95 Traffic Safety Supply. Customer names are not released to any outside party unless required for the purpose of ordering and/or delivery of order.

Billing information consists of information required to bill an order the customer has placed. The billing information is retained as required by law for accounting reasons. This information is not released to any outside party.

Contact information consists of information the customer has provided to 95TrafficSafety.com for staff to contact them regarding a question or an order. This information is retained for use by 95 Traffic Safety Supply team members. Contact information is not released to any outside party unless specifically requested by the customer.

95TrafficSafety.com gathers user information in the following ways:

Contests and sweepstakes - 95TrafficSafety.com offers contests and sweepstakes in which we request certain personal information. At a minimum, the required information includes name and email address; in some cases, the user must provide a shipping address for the winning prize to be sent to.

Usage Tracking 95TrafficSafety.com tracks user traffic patterns throughout all of our sites. 95TrafficSafety.com sometimes tracks and catalogs the search terms that customers enter in our Search function, but this tracking is never associated with individual customers.

Cookies A cookie is a small data file that certain Websites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages you've visited. But the only personal information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. 95TrafficSafety.com uses cookies to track user traffic patterns (as described above), when you click an ad.

Ads 95TrafficSafety.com's advertising system delivers a one-time cookie to better track ad impressions and click rates. If you've set your browser to warn you before accepting cookies, you will receive the warning message with each cookie. You can refuse cookies by turning them off in your browser. You do not need to have cookies turned on to use any 95TrafficSafety.com site. However, you do need cookies to participate in some of 95TrafficSafety.com's affiliate programs, but that is clearly explained at the time of acceptance of that program.

Electronic Newsletters 95TrafficSafety.com offers newsletters to customers who choose to sign up for this service. 95TrafficSafety.com gathers the email addresses of customers who voluntarily subscribe. 95TrafficSafety.com delivers newsletters via email to only registered recipients from our newsletter sign up page.

The email tracking system recognizes the URL that was clicked and records some information about you and your computers, such as the email address that you provided to us, the browser, operating system, and IP address. 95TrafficSafety.com also uses the feature of your browser to set a "cookie" on your system. This allows us to measure and report the click through rate on emails delivered and then better determine future content.
