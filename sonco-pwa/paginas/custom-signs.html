What Makes SONCO Crowd Control
Barriers Different?

Every crowd control barrier that you find at the SONCO store was developed in-house based on field research, industry feedback, and extensive testing for almost half a century. We don’t go based on the minimum guidelines – our goal is to promote safety first, setting the standard for quality crowd control barriers so you avoid any unnecessary liability.
