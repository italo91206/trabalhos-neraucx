SAFE SHOPPING GUARANTEE


Shopping at Sonco is Safer.

We understand that security is essential when buying online. That's why we have worked hard to create one of the safest online shopping environments in the industry.

Our checkout process is 100% secure.

Your order information is encrypted and transmitted over our secure (SSL) server technology.

We pride ourselves on order accuracy.

From the moment you place your order until it leaves our warehouse, if we see something that's not clear, we'll contact you BEFORE we charge your credit card. We want it to be right, the first time

We take fraud very seriously.

We take extra steps to carefully validate all credit card information. You can be secure in the knowledge that we're protecting you from unauthorized charges. And, by keeping fraud down, we can also keep prices down.

Don't worry about identity theft

Only the people responsible for processing your order see your personal information.

Packages arrive safely.

When your order ships, we go an extra step to protect your packages in transit. Our exclusive Sonco system protects your shipment from being "hijacked" by protecting the tracking number. You can log in anytime, day or night, to track your order in real time.

You are protected.

In the unlikely event that an unauthorized charge does occur, the Fair Credit Act guarantees you zero liability for any loss. Please notify us promptly upon receipt of your credit card statement.

Sonco Price Match Terms & Conditions

  • Evidence of advertised price must be provided or easily available.
  • Item being price matched must be new and be an identical brand, make and model, must be in stock and available for sale.
  • Price match 5-day guarantee is valid from the date of request. Excludes finance sales.
  • In order to qualify, products must be like for like with regards to specification, colour & condition. E&OE
  • We must be able to verify stocks.

Offer excludes

  • Prices available at online only retailers, auction sites, classifieds, community marketplaces, and outlet shops.
  • Price Beat excludes special orders.
  • Prices offered by importers or overseas retailers in currency other than US dollars, that is, where the product will require delivery.
  • Priced in error, that is, where we or a competitor have advertised the wrong price by mistake.
