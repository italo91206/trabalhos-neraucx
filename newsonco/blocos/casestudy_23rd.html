HOW 23RD VETERAN MANAGES
RACE FLOW AND RAISES FUNDS
TO SUPPORT OUR VETERANS

23rd Veteran is a groundbreaking program that helps
veterans adjust back to the civilian world. They also
provide training to businesses and communities who
want to create a more positive environment for our
returning heroes.
