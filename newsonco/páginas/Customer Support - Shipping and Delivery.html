FREE Shipping

Sonco has the best shipping deal on the planet: FREE SHIPPING* with no minimum purchase, on selected products, all courtesy of Sonco.

*Certain items, such as special order or oversized may not qualify for free shipping.

Additional Shipping Options

If your order does not qualify for free shipping – or if you require faster delivery – you will still enjoy very reasonable shipping rates. After entering your Zip Code on the Sonco checkout page, we'll show you your shipping options and charges. Just select the method you prefer.

In certain situations (such as unusually large or heavy items), we'll work with you to determine the most effective and economical shipping method. Call us at (888) 766-2615 for assistance.

Delivery Times

Thanks to special arrangements with our principal carriers, we have the latest same-day shipping cut-off time in the industry.

International Shipping

We ship anywhere in the world. Contact our sales team at (888) 766-2615 for more information.

Check Your Order Status Online

Sonco can track your order for you and give you up-to-the minute information on its location. It's secure and convenient way to keep tabs on your order all the way from our door to yours. We want you to enjoy your Sonco experience from start to finish!
