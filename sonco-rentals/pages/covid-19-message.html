Helping to Prevent the Spread

As the current emergency situation changes, our fulfillment teams are continually updating operating procedures to protect our associates and customers from the spread of this virus.

• If anyone on our staff feels ill or is exhibiting any of the symptoms of the COVID-19 virus, they have been instructed to immediately go home and seek medical assistance. Under no circumstances is an employee to remain in the workplace or on jobsites if they are not feeling well.   
• We have equipped our Frontline Teams with sanitizers and disinfectant wipes, we’ve asked them to wash their hands regularly and avoid close contact with team members and the public (maintain social distancing of 6 feet). We’ve also instituted a “no handshake policy” indefinitely.
• We will be limiting the number of crew members in our company trucks to accommodate proper social distancing.
• Should you require our services, please let us know if you have any special requests or requirements for our staff on your sites, so we maintain a safe workplace for all.
